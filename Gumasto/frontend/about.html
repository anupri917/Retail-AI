About Gumasto

Transforming Indian Retail through AI-powered Intelligence.

Our Journey

Gumasto was born from a simple truth — most Indian retailers don’t fail because of lack of effort, they fail because of lack of insight. Every day, shop owners lose money due to expired products, poor stock planning, and gut-based decisions. We saw hardworking retailers doing everything right — except having the data they deserved. Founded in 2025 by a team of technologists and retail problem-solvers, Gumasto was built with one clear mission: to turn raw shop data into intelligent business decisions. What started as a small idea during late-night brainstorming sessions quickly evolved into a powerful AI-driven retail intelligence platform. By combining machine learning, inventory science, and real-world retail experience, we created a system that doesn’t just show numbers — it thinks like a gumasta. Today, Gumasto is helping retailers reduce waste, increase profit margins, and operate smarter every single day — not by guessing, but by trusting data.
